# AI Tools & Platforms

Evaluations, comparisons, and guidance on AI tools and platforms for business use.

## Categories

- Large language models (LLMs)
- AI development platforms
- MLOps & LLMOps tools
- AI agent frameworks
- AutoML & no-code AI
- Data labelling & preparation
- Model monitoring & observability
- Vector databases & RAG
- AI safety & guardrails
- Cloud AI services (AWS, Azure, GCP)

## Evaluation Criteria

- Business readiness
- Pricing & licensing
- Security & compliance
- Integration complexity
- Vendor lock-in risk
